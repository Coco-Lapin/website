Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom jewelry take?

Custom pieces typically take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. We'll provide a timeline when you submit your design.

Do you offer repairs?

Yes! We specialize in jewelry repair for all types of pieces. Bring them in for a free assessment.

What materials do you use?

We use ethically sourced gold, silver, and precious gemstones. All materials are certified authentic.

Do you have a warranty?

All our pieces come with a lifetime warranty on craftsmanship and materials.
